Save Beau The Dog!

Dog Sentenced to Death For Killing Duck

Beau, a 2 and a 1/2 year old Golden Retriever mix has been declared viscious by a judge in Dyersburg, Tennessee. Beau attacked and killed a duck that wandered onto his owner's property. Animal Control was contacted, and Beau was taken away to boarding, where he is to stay until his scheduled euthanization on November 20th.

Beau has no history of aggression or biting. A Dyersburg judge deemed him as a "vicious animal". All the while Beau's owner, Danny Higgins, is paying for Beau to be kept in boarding until he is either released or euthanized next month.
